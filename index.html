Frequently
asked questions

01

What exactly is a "positive reply"?

A positive reply is when a prospect responds with genuine interest — something like "yes, tell me more" or "let's set up a call." We never count out-of-office replies, unsubscribes, or neutral responses. You only pay when someone actually wants to talk.

02

How long before I start seeing results?

Most clients see their first positive replies within 2–3 weeks of campaign launch. We spend the first week on setup, research, and domain warmup — then campaigns go live and results follow quickly.

03

Do you work with any industry or niche?

We've worked across 38+ industries including SaaS, real estate, recruitment, e-commerce, and professional services. Before we take you on as a client, we assess whether your offer and target audience are a good fit for cold outreach.

04

What do I need to get started?

Just a clear description of your ideal client, your offer, and a sending domain. We handle everything else — research, copywriting, sending infrastructure, and reporting. You just show up for the replies.

05

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. There are no long-term contracts. You pay monthly and can cancel anytime. We're confident enough in our results that we don't need to lock you in.
